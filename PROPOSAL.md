## üé® Design Direction (Moodboard)

**Concept:** "Elevated Humble"
Merging the grounded, community feel of an "Angkringan" with the precision of a modern coffee brand.

**Visual Identity:**
- **Colors:** Deep Taupe (Grounding), Olive Green (Organic), Burnt Cream (Warmth).
- **Typography:** Sans-serif, geometric but human (e.g., DM Sans, Inter).
- **Vibe:** Clean, Accessible, Unpretentious, Warm.

**Inspiration:**
- Abstract cues from *Kopi Tuku* (friendly, neighborhood vibe) but with a more spacious, "journal-like" layout.
- *Japanese Kissaten* influences (craft, silence).
